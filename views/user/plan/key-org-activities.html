It is essential for the BCMS project manager to understand and know the range of products and services of the organization. The types of goods and services produced by the organization will have a major impact on its business model and how the organization conducts its business. Products and services may expose the organization to specific risks such as environmental hazards.

At this stage do not complete a comprehensive process mapping exercise or detailed inventory of assets. These details will be captured later. A general list is sufficient here.
