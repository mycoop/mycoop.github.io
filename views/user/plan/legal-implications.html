Legal and Regulatory Compliance: Access Implications

The BC Manager is responsible for ensuring that a full assessment of the implications of specified regulatory and/or compliance requirements is conducted. This will be based upon consulting the responsible parties identified in the Legal and Regulatory Compliance Access section. The assessment should result in the following information:
  • Impact to consider for BCMS Policy (Degree of change to the BCMS and its associated policies strategies and plans needed to meet the requirement)
  • Urgency of meeting the requirement
  • Consequences of not meeting the requirement
  • Options for meeting the requirement
