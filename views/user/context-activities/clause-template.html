Area of ISO 22301 Addressed: clause 4.1 Understanding of the Organization and its Context

It is necessary to obtain an overview of the organization to understand the compliance, challenges, of the organization and the inherent risk in that market segment. General information about the related organization should be collected to better appreciate its mission, strategies, purpose, etc. This helps ensuring consistency and alignment between the strategic objectives for risk management and the organization's mission.
